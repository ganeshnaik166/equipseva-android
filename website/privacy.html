Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-25

1. Who we are

EquipSeva ("we", "us", "our") is the operator of the EquipSeva platform — Android, iOS, and web — connecting hospitals with KYC-verified biomedical engineers in India. Grievance officer details: support@equipseva.com.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data — name, phone, email, role (hospital / engineer).
  • KYC data (engineers only) — Aadhaar number (masked), PAN, live selfie. Encrypted at rest.
  • Job data — equipment photos, service address, fault descriptions, chat messages, ratings.
  • Device data — Android/iOS version, app version, crash logs, push token.
  • Location data — only with permission, only while you have a job in progress, and only at the precision needed to match nearby engineers.

3. Why we collect it

To run the marketplace: matching engineers and jobs, verifying identities, processing payments, sending push notifications, resolving disputes, and complying with Indian tax and consumer protection law.

4. Sharing

We don't sell your data. We share it only with: (a) the counterparty on a job (limited profile and contact while a job is active), (b) infrastructure providers under contract (cloud, KYC, payments), and (c) authorities when legally compelled.

5. Security

Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+), encryption at rest, role-based access internally, and Supabase Row-Level Security on every database table. Security disclosures: security@equipseva.com.

6. Your rights

  • Access — see all your data in-app.
  • Correction — edit profile, fix errors at any time.
  • Deletion — Settings → Account → Delete account. Personal data and KYC documents are purged within 30 days; tax-required job records retained for 7 years per Indian law.
  • Withdraw consent — revoke camera/location/notifications in OS settings.

7. Children

EquipSeva is not directed at children under 18. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us and we will delete the account.

8. Changes

We post material changes here and notify registered users in-app at least 7 days before they take effect.

9. Contact

Questions, complaints, or rights requests: support@equipseva.com.
