FAQ

Pricing questions

Will EquipSeva always be free?

v1 is free for both hospitals and engineers. We'll introduce a small per-job platform fee in v2 — fully transparent, opt-in for chains, and announced months in advance.

Is there a setup or onboarding fee?

No. Hospitals and engineers can sign up, complete KYC, and start posting or bidding immediately at no cost.

Are there charges for chains?

The base product stays free. Chains can opt into a custom plan with SLAs and consolidated invoicing — pricing depends on number of sites and monthly volume.

Do engineers pay commission?

Not in v1. Engineers keep 100% of every bid amount. v2 will introduce a small flat per-job fee, capped and disclosed up front.
