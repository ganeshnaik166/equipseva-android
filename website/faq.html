For hospitals

Hospital & admin questions

How fast can I get an engineer on site?

Most repair requests get verified bids within 30 minutes during business hours; same-day on-site arrival is typical inside metros and often within 2 hours.

Which equipment can EquipSeva cover?

Patient monitors, OT lights, ultrasound probes, autoclaves, defibrillators, ECG, X-ray, ventilators, anaesthesia workstations, suction units, centrifuges, infusion pumps — and most hospital and diagnostic equipment beyond that list.

How do you stop unverified people walking into my hospital?

No engineer can bid on a single job until they pass Aadhaar + PAN + live selfie KYC. The hospital sees their verified profile before accepting any bid.

Can multiple users from my hospital use the same account?

Yes. Hospital chains get role-based access — procurement, biomedical lead, and finance each get their own scope.

What if the engineer doesn't show up?

Bids carry an ETA commitment. Missed ETAs flag automatically; you can cancel and re-post without penalty, and the engineer's rating reflects it.

For engineers

Biomedical engineer questions

How long does KYC take?

Most KYC submissions clear within minutes during business hours — Aadhaar OTP, PAN check, and a live selfie. Edge cases occasionally take up to 24 hours for manual review.

Do I pay any commission?

Not in v1. You keep 100% of every bid amount. We will introduce a small flat per-job fee in v2, capped and disclosed up front.

How do I get paid?

UPI to your linked bank account, the moment a job is closed with photo proof and a hospital signature.

Can I work in multiple cities?

Yes. Set your service area on a 50 km map and update it any time. Hospitals only see engineers whose radius covers the job site.

Privacy

Data, security, and account

How do I delete my account?

In-app: Settings → Account → Delete account. We delete personal data and KYC documents within 30 days, retaining only legal-minimum tax records.

Is my data sold or shared?

Never. We don't sell user data. KYC documents are encrypted at rest and only accessed for verification and dispute resolution.

Where is my data stored?

EquipSeva stores user data in India-region data centres operated by reputable infrastructure providers, with encryption in transit and at rest.
