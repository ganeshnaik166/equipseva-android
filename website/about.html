A working OT, every day, in every Indian hospital.

EquipSeva exists because hospital equipment downtime is the silent enemy of Indian healthcare — and the fix has been stuck in phone trees and WhatsApp groups for too long.

Our mission

One trusted layer for hospital ops.

Hospitals in India deserve the same on-demand reliability that consumers get from food delivery, ride hailing, and home services. Verified people. Real-time tracking. Photo proof. UPI payouts. We're building that layer for biomedical equipment first — and parts and procurement next.

  • Verified by default — KYC before any engineer can bid
  • Privacy by default — encrypted everywhere, deletable anytime
  • Free by default — no platform fee in v1
  • Transparent always — ratings, receipts, photos on every job
