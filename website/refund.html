Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-25

1. Cancelling a posted job (hospital)

Hospitals can cancel a posted job free of charge until a bid is accepted. After acceptance and before the engineer starts travelling to site, cancellation incurs no fee. After the engineer is en route or has arrived, a cancellation fee equal to the bid's travel component (or a flat ₹300, whichever is lower) may apply, paid directly to the engineer.

2. Withdrawing a bid (engineer)

Engineers can withdraw bids any time before acceptance. After acceptance, repeated withdrawals affect ratings and may result in suspension. Engineers must communicate genuine emergencies to support immediately.

3. Failed service

If an engineer fails to perform the agreed service or causes damage, the hospital may open a dispute in-app within 7 days of the job's scheduled close. Refunds are processed via the original UPI rail within 7 working days of dispute resolution in the hospital's favour.

4. Engineer no-show

If an engineer misses the committed ETA by more than 60 minutes without prior communication, the hospital may cancel without penalty and the engineer's rating reflects the miss. Hospitals are not charged.

5. Platform refunds

EquipSeva v1 does not charge a platform fee, so platform refunds are not applicable. If we introduce platform fees in v2, refund terms will be added here before fees take effect.

6. Dispute mediation

Open disputes in-app: Job → Help → Open dispute. Decisions are made within 5 working days using job photos, chat history, and ratings.

7. Contact

For refund queries: support@equipseva.com.
