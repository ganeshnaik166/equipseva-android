Terms of Service

Last updated: 2026-04-25

1. The platform

EquipSeva is a marketplace that connects hospitals (requesters) with verified biomedical engineers (service providers). EquipSeva does not perform repairs itself; it facilitates discovery, communication, and payment.

2. Account registration

Users must be 18+ and provide accurate identity information. Engineers must complete KYC (Aadhaar, PAN, live selfie) before bidding. Hospitals are responsible for ensuring their staff use the platform within their authority.

3. Job conduct

  • Hospitals provide accurate fault details, photos, and access.
  • Engineers provide accurate ETAs, professional service, and photo-proof closure.
  • Both parties must use in-app communication; off-platform side deals are at your own risk.

4. Payments

Hospitals pay engineers on job closure via UPI/Razorpay-backed flows. EquipSeva v1 does not charge a platform fee. Future fees will be disclosed in advance and require continued use as acceptance.

5. Disputes

Open a dispute in-app within 7 days of job closure. EquipSeva mediates using job photos, chat history, and ratings. Decisions are final for platform purposes; users retain all legal rights outside the platform.

6. Prohibited conduct

  • Misrepresenting identity, qualifications, or KYC documents.
  • Posting non-equipment-related jobs (e.g. unrelated services).
  • Harassment, abuse, or violations of patient privacy.
  • Reverse-engineering or scraping the platform.

7. Intellectual property

EquipSeva owns the platform, branding, and software. Users retain ownership of content they upload (photos, text), granting EquipSeva a licence to host and display it for service operation.

8. Liability

EquipSeva is not liable for service quality of independent engineers. Engineers are not employees of EquipSeva. Maximum aggregate liability of EquipSeva to any user is limited to fees paid to EquipSeva in the prior 12 months — which in v1, is zero.

9. Termination

We may suspend accounts for breach (failed KYC, repeated low ratings, fraudulent activity). Users may close accounts any time in-app.

10. Governing law

These terms are governed by Indian law. Disputes are subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Hyderabad, Telangana.
