From fault to fix in four steps.

Designed for the speed Indian hospitals actually need — most repairs see a verified engineer on site the same day.

  1. Post the repair

    Photos, brand, model, fault description, and service address. Auto-suggested fields keep posting under 60 seconds, even on hospital 4G.

  2. Get verified bids

    KYC-verified biomedical engineers nearby bid with price, ETA, and ratings. You see specialisations and brands serviced before picking.

  3. Track the engineer

    Live on-the-way status, on-site check-in, and work progress — every event push-notified to your team.

  4. Close with photo proof

    Before-and-after photos, signed completion, transparent rating, and on-completion payout via UPI.
