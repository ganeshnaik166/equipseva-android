FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

How fast can I get an engineer on site?

Most repair requests receive bids from KYC-verified engineers within 30 minutes during business hours, and on-site arrival typically follows in under two hours within metros.

Which equipment does EquipSeva cover?

Patient monitors, OT lights, ultrasound probes, autoclaves, defibrillators, ECG, X-ray, ventilators, anaesthesia workstations, suction units, centrifuges, and infusion pumps — and most hospital and diagnostic equipment beyond that list.

How are engineers verified?

Aadhaar, PAN, and a live selfie KYC happen before an engineer can bid on a single job. Specialisations and brands serviced are reviewed by our team.

Is EquipSeva free?

Yes. v1 is free for both hospitals and engineers — no platform fee, no subscriptions, no hidden charges.

Which cities do you cover?

EquipSeva is rolling out city by city across India. The biggest engineer pools today are in Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi NCR, Kolkata, and Ahmedabad.

Is the app on iOS too?

Android is live. iOS is in private beta — drop your email below to be invited.
