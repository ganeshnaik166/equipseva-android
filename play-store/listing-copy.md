## Full description (max 4000)

```
EquipSeva is the fastest way for Indian hospitals, clinics, and diagnostic
centres to get medical equipment repaired by verified biomedical engineers.

Whether it's an OT light, a patient monitor, an ultrasound probe, an autoclave,
or a defibrillator that won't hold charge, EquipSeva connects you with a
KYC-verified engineer in your city in minutes — not days.

— FOR HOSPITALS & BIOMEDICAL DEPARTMENTS —

• Post a repair job in under 60 seconds. Add equipment photos, brand, model,
  fault description, and your service address.
• Receive bids from nearby engineers, complete with pricing, ETA, and ratings.
• Pick an engineer, track their on-the-way status, and confirm completion with
  before/after photos uploaded directly inside the app.
• Call, WhatsApp, or email the engineer directly — no middlemen, no call
  centres.
• Build an address book of every site you operate so repeat jobs take seconds.
• Bid + status updates work even on flaky hospital Wi-Fi: actions queue offline
  and sync the moment you're back on a signal.

— FOR BIOMEDICAL ENGINEERS —

• Set your service area on a 50 km map and get push notifications the instant
  a matching job opens nearby.
• Build a verified profile with your specialisations, certifications, brands
  serviced, and past work — KYC-checked so hospitals trust you on day one.
• Bid on jobs, chat with the requester, and track everything from a single
  dashboard.
• Get paid on completion, with transparent ratings and reviews.

— TRUST & SAFETY —

• KYC verification before any engineer can bid: Aadhaar, PAN, and a live
  selfie. Hospital procurement teams complete a separate KYC before
  placing high-value jobs.
• End-to-end encryption in transit and at rest for every chat, KYC document,
  and job photo.
• In-app reporting and one-tap account deletion any time.

— BUILT FOR INDIAN HOSPITAL REALITY —

• Works on 4G with poor indoor reception — most flows are offline-safe.
• Push notifications for every status change so the engineer never misses a
  job and the hospital is never left guessing.
• Multi-site support for chains, with role-based access for procurement
  managers and biomedical leads.

v1 ships with hospitals + biomedical engineers; marketplace for spare parts
coming in v2.

Questions or feedback: support@equipseva.com
```
