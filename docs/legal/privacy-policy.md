This Privacy Policy explains how the EquipSeva mobile application and related backend services (collectively, the "Service") collect, use, share, and protect your personal information. It is written in line with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP) of India and complies with the Google Play Store data-safety disclosure rules.

By creating an account or using the Service you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, do not use the Service.

## 1. Who we are

EquipSeva is a marketplace that connects Indian hospitals with field engineers, parts suppliers, equipment manufacturers, and logistics partners for the sourcing and servicing of medical equipment. The Service is operated as a sole-proprietorship at this time; an Indian private limited entity is in formation.

## 2. Categories of data we collect

| Category | Examples | Source | Required? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Account** | Email, phone number, full name, hashed password, Supabase user id | You provide on sign-up | Yes |
| **Profile** | Avatar image, role (hospital/engineer/supplier/manufacturer/logistics), organization id and name | You provide; some via OAuth | Yes |
| **Verification (KYC)** | Government ID photo, professional licence photo, equipment categories you service, your geographic service area | You upload as an engineer or supplier | Only if you opt into a verified-seller / verified-engineer role |

We do **not** collect: contacts, photos library beyond what you explicitly select, browsing history outside the app, biometric data, financial card details (Razorpay handles those — we never see card numbers), any data from minors under 18 (the Service is not directed at children).

## 3. Why we collect each category (purposes)

- **Account & Profile** — to give you a stable identity, route role-specific features, and let counterparties contact you.
- **Verification (KYC) & Business** — to keep the marketplace safe (only verified suppliers can list parts; only verified engineers can take repair jobs); to issue legally-compliant tax invoices.
- **Transactions** — to fulfil orders, settle payouts, comply with tax law (GST), and produce audit-trail records.
- **Operational** — to deliver the core marketplace + chat + RFQ + ratings features.
- **Device** — to deliver push notifications, prevent abuse via Play Integrity, and detect repackaged APKs via signature verification.
- **Crash & diagnostics** — to identify and fix bugs.
- **Location** — to match engineers and hospitals by geographic proximity.

We rely primarily on the **contractual necessity** and **legitimate interest** legal bases under the DPDP. For optional features (KYC docs, location, push notifications) we rely on your explicit **consent**, which you can withdraw any time from Profile → Settings.

We may disclose data **without your consent** only to comply with a binding court order, statute, or to investigate serious abuse. We will inform you within seven days of any such disclosure unless prohibited by law.

We do **not** sell your personal data. We do **not** use it for advertising or profiling. We do not run ad networks inside the app.

## 5. International transfers

Some of our processors store data outside India (USA, EU). Where this happens we rely on the standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards each provider publishes. Aggregate medical-equipment marketplace data does not include personal medical records, so data-localisation rules under upcoming DPDP rules are unlikely to apply, but we will adjust storage residency if the rules change.

## 6. How long we keep data

| Category | Retention |
|---|---|
| Account, Profile | Until you delete your account; then deleted within 30 days |
| KYC documents | Until you delete your account or until 3 years after the verification was last used, whichever is later (regulatory / fraud-prevention) |
| Transactions, invoices | 7 years (tax law) |
| Chat messages | Until either party soft-deletes; 90 days post-deletion in cold storage for moderation appeals |
| Crash & diagnostics | 90 days |
| Device tokens | Until logout, app uninstall, or token rotation |

You can delete your account any time from Profile → Account → Delete account. This triggers our `delete_my_account` server-side RPC, which removes or anonymises rows across all tables in a single transaction. Tax-mandated rows (orders, invoices) are anonymised — buyer ids replaced with a tombstone — but the order line items are retained for the statutory period.

## 7. Your rights

Under the DPDP and equivalent laws you have the right to:

- **Access** — see what we store about you. Use Profile → Account → Export my data; we generate a JSON bundle on demand.
- **Correction** — fix wrong information from Profile and the per-role sub-screens.
- **Erasure** — delete your account; tax-mandated retention applies as above.
- **Withdraw consent** — turn off KYC, location, push notifications from Profile → Settings.
- **Grievance** — write to the email below; we will respond within 7 days.

## 8. Security

- All traffic between the app and our backend is encrypted with TLS 1.2+.
- The on-device database is encrypted at rest using SQLCipher with a key stored in the Android Keystore (non-extractable).
- Server-side data is encrypted at rest by Supabase.
- Row-level-security (RLS) policies on every Postgres table enforce that you can only read or modify rows that belong to you.
- Sensitive operations (placing an order, KYC submit) are gated by Play Integrity attestation when the device claims to be a Play-installed Android device.

No system is perfect. If we discover a breach we will notify affected users within 72 hours via email and an in-app banner.

## 9. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the Service please email us and we will delete the account.
