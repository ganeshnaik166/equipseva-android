This Refund Policy explains your refund, return, and cancellation rights when transacting on EquipSeva. It is part of our [Terms of Service](/terms) and is referenced in §9 of the Terms.

## 1. Scope

EquipSeva facilitates three types of paid transactions:

1. **Spare-part orders** — you buy parts or equipment from a verified Supplier and pay via Razorpay.
2. **Repair-service jobs** — you accept an Engineer's bid for a repair, with payment held until job completion.
3. **Equipment listings (Buy/Sell)** — same flow as spare-part orders for larger equipment.

Refund mechanics differ across these categories and are described below.

## 2. Cancellation by the Buyer

### Spare-part / Equipment orders

- You may cancel any order **before** it has been marked "Shipped" by the Supplier from Orders → Order detail → Cancel order. We require a brief reason; this is shared with the Supplier.
- If the order has not been shipped, the full amount is refunded to your original payment method within 5–7 working days.
- If the order is already shipped or in transit, cancellation is not possible from the app; treat it as a return (see §3).

### Repair-service jobs

- You may cancel an accepted bid **before** the Engineer has been dispatched (status `assigned`) at no charge.
- After dispatch (`in_transit` / `awaiting_completion`) cancellation is at the Engineer's discretion. A travel charge (as agreed in chat) may apply.

## 3. Returns

You may request a return on a **delivered** spare-part / equipment order within **48 hours of delivery** if:

- the item arrived damaged or non-functional;
- the wrong item was sent;
- the item materially differs from the listing (brand, model, certification).

Open Order detail → Report issue. Photo evidence is required. Once the Supplier (or, on appeal, our admin team) approves the return, you ship the item back. Once the Supplier confirms receipt and inspection, the refund is initiated.

Items that cannot be returned (unless damaged or wrong-shipped):

- consumable / single-use items already used or with broken seals;
- bespoke or custom-spec items (e.g. branded MRO ordered against a specific serial);
- items where the buyer has installed, modified, or attempted self-repair.

## 4. Refund processing time

- We initiate refund within **3 working days** of approval.
- Razorpay credits the original payment instrument within **5–7 working days** for cards, **2–3 working days** for UPI / netbanking, and up to **10 working days** for international cards.
- For cash-on-delivery (when offered) refunds are processed via NEFT to a bank account you provide; please allow **7 working days**.

We never refund to a different person or a different account than the one used for the original payment.

## 5. Disputes

If you believe an order, bid, or refund was handled incorrectly:

1. Try to resolve directly with the counterparty via in-app chat.
2. If unresolved, open Order detail → Report a problem; this escalates to our admin queue.
3. Our admin team will review evidence (chat history, photos, delivery confirmations, payment trail) and respond within **7 working days** with a binding decision under these Terms.
4. Decisions of our admin team are final but do not waive any statutory remedy you may have under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, or the equivalent local law.

## 6. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your card issuer or bank without first attempting resolution through the app, we may suspend your account pending the dispute's outcome. Frivolous chargebacks may result in permanent termination.

## 7. Fraudulent claims

Fraudulent return or refund claims (e.g. claiming non-delivery when the parcel was delivered, fake damage photos) are grounds for immediate account termination, forfeiture of pending payouts, and referral to law enforcement.

## 8. Statutory rights

Nothing in this Policy limits your rights under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, the Sale of Goods Act, 1930, or any other Indian law that applies to the underlying transaction.
