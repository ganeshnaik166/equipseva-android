# Refund and Cancellation Policy — EquipSeva

**Effective:** 2026-04-25
**Last updated:** 2026-04-25

This policy explains when you can cancel an order or service on EquipSeva and how refunds work. It is required by the RBI and Razorpay's merchant terms, and it forms part of the [Terms of Service](./TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md).

We process all refunds through Razorpay back to the original payment method.

---

## 1. Spare-part orders (marketplace)

### When you can cancel for a full refund
- Before the supplier marks the order as "Packed" or "Shipped" — cancel from Orders → tap order → Cancel. A free-text reason is optional.
- After "Shipped" but before delivery: only with the supplier's consent through in-App chat.

### When you are entitled to a refund after delivery
- The item is **defective on arrival** (DOA). Report within 48 hours of delivery with photos through Orders → Report Issue.
- The item received is **wrong** (different SKU, different model, different specification).
- The item is **not delivered** despite a "Delivered" status (raise a dispute within 7 days).

### When you are NOT entitled to a refund
- You changed your mind after delivery.
- You did not read the listing description / specification.
- The item was damaged after delivery by handling, installation, or use.
- Hospital-grade consumables (sterile, single-use) once the seal is broken.

### Refund timelines
- We initiate refunds within 2 business days of approving the request.
- Razorpay typically credits the refund to your card / UPI / bank in 5–7 business days. Some banks may take longer; in that case Razorpay's reference number is your tracking ID.

---

## 2. Repair-service jobs (engineer payouts)

EquipSeva uses an **escrow-on-acceptance** model for repair jobs:

- When you accept a bid, the bid amount is captured from your payment method into an escrow held by EquipSeva.
- Funds release to the engineer only when **you mark the job complete and confirm satisfaction** in the App.
- If the engineer does not show up, abandons the job, or the job is not completed within the agreed window, you can raise a dispute. After investigation we refund the escrow to you.
- Either party may cancel before the engineer is en route at no charge. After "On the way" status, cancellation may attract a travel + diagnostic fee disclosed in the bid.

---

## 3. RFQs and supplier bids

- Quotations on RFQs are non-binding until you formally accept a bid.
- Once you accept, the rules in §1 (spare-part order) apply.

---

## 4. Platform fees

EquipSeva's platform fee (where applicable) is non-refundable except where the order is cancelled fully due to our fault (e.g. App outage that prevented delivery confirmation).

---

## 5. How to request a refund

1. Open the App → Orders or Repair Jobs.
2. Tap the order/job → Report Issue or Cancel.
3. Choose a reason and (for after-delivery issues) attach photos.
4. We respond within 5 business days, usually faster.

For escalation, contact:
- support@equipseva.com
- Grievance Officer: see [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md) §1

---

## 6. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your bank or card network without first contacting us, we reserve the right to suspend the account pending investigation. Most chargebacks are quicker to resolve through us directly.

---

## 7. Updates

We will notify you in-App at least 7 days before material changes. The version effective on the date of your transaction governs that transaction.
