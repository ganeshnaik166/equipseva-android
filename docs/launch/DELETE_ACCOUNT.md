# Delete your EquipSeva account

This page explains how to request deletion of your **EquipSeva** account and what happens to your data.

EquipSeva is published by Ganesh Dhanavath. The Android app is distributed via Google Play as `com.equipseva.app`.

## How to delete your account in the app

You can delete your account directly from inside the EquipSeva Android app at any time:

1. Open the **EquipSeva** app and sign in.
2. Go to **Profile** (bottom navigation, right tab).
3. Tap **Personal info** → scroll to the bottom.
4. Tap **Delete my account** → confirm with your password.

Your account becomes immediately inaccessible. A confirmation email is sent to your registered address.

## How to delete your account without the app

If you can no longer sign in or have already uninstalled the app, email us at **ganesh1431.dhanavath@gmail.com** from the email address registered on your EquipSeva account. Subject line: `Account deletion request — EquipSeva`. Include your registered phone number for verification.

We action the request within **7 working days** and send a confirmation email once complete.

## What gets deleted

Within **30 days** of the deletion request, we permanently delete:

- Your profile (name, email, phone, address, role).
- Your KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN, ID photos) — engineers only.
- Your in-app messages and chat history.
- Your repair-job posts, bids, and reviews authored by you.
- Photos you uploaded (KYC, repair photos).
- Your push-notification token.
- Crash logs and diagnostics attributable to you in Sentry and Firebase Crashlytics.

## What is retained, and for how long

A small set of records are retained for legal and accounting compliance:

| Data | Retained for | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Razorpay payment transaction records | **8 years** | RBI / Income Tax Act compliance for financial transactions |
| GST invoices issued to or by you | **8 years** | Income Tax Act §44AA + GST Act compliance |
| Anonymised aggregated usage metrics (no personal identifiers) | **Indefinitely** | Product analytics — cannot be linked back to you |
| Audit logs of the deletion request itself | **2 years** | DPDP Act §11 record-keeping |

These records do **not** contain your full name, contact details, or any document images — only the legally-required transaction metadata (amount, date, payment reference, tax identifiers).
