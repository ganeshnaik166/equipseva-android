# IARC Content Rating answers — EquipSeva

When Play Console runs the IARC questionnaire, use these answers. Expected outcome: **Rated for 3+ / Everyone** with a **User-Generated Content** flag.

---

## Violence

- Does the app contain violence? **No**
- Realistic / cartoon / fantasy violence? **No**
- Blood, gore, dismemberment? **No**
- Use of weapons? **No**

## Fear

- Frightening or disturbing content? **No**
- Horror imagery? **No**

## Sexual content

- Sexual themes / nudity / suggestive content? **No**
- Sexually-explicit material? **No**

## Profanity

- Crude language built into the app? **No** (all in-app strings are professional product copy)
- User-generated text that may contain profanity? **Yes** (chat between users)
- Mitigation: in-app **report** + **block** controls; banned-account enforcement.

## Drugs / alcohol / tobacco

- References to drugs, alcohol, or tobacco? **No**

## Gambling

- Real-money gambling? **No**
- Simulated gambling? **No**

## User-generated content

- Does the app allow users to interact with each other or share content? **Yes**
  - In-app chat between buyers and sellers / hospitals and engineers.
  - Listings, RFQs, bids, reviews — all user-generated.
- Is there a moderation mechanism? **Yes** — report content, block user, content-policy enforcement, KYC for high-trust roles.

## Location sharing

- Does the app share user location with other users? **Yes — but only the textual shipping address provided at checkout, with the seller of that order, for fulfilment.** No precise GPS or background location.

## Personal info sharing

- Does the app collect or share personal info? **Yes** — see Data Safety form.
- Is the privacy policy linked? **Yes** — required URL provided in Play Console.

## Digital purchases

- In-app purchases? **Yes — physical goods and services paid via Razorpay.** Note: this is **not** Google Play Billing (which is required for digital goods only). Razorpay is permitted under Play's billing policy because we sell physical goods and real-world services, not in-app digital content.

## Mature themes / hateful content / gore

- Hate speech, discrimination, extremism? **No**
- Self-harm, suicide content? **No**
- Glorification of crime? **No**
